Our Services

What We Provide

  • Root Canal

    A root canal is a dental procedure used to treat infection at the center of a tooth (the pulp). The procedure involves removing the infected or damaged pulp, cleaning and disinfecting the inside of the tooth, and then filling and sealing it. Root canals are often necessary when a tooth is severely decayed or infected, and they can help save the tooth from extraction.

  • Alignment Teeth

    Teeth alignment refers to the positioning of teeth in proper relation to each other and the jawline. Misaligned teeth, such as those that are crooked, overcrowded, or have gaps, can be corrected using orthodontic treatments like braces or clear aligners (e.g., Invisalign). Proper alignment of teeth is important for maintaining oral health, improving appearance, and ensuring efficient chewing and speech.

  • Cosmetic Teeth

    Cosmetic teeth refer to various dental treatments and procedures aimed at improving the appearance of a person's teeth and smile. This can include teeth whitening, veneers, crowns, bonding, and reshaping. Cosmetic dentistry focuses on enhancing the aesthetic aspect of teeth, helping individuals achieve a more attractive smile.

  • Oral Hygiene

    Oral hygiene is the practice of keeping the mouth, teeth, and gums clean and healthy to prevent dental issues like cavities, gum disease, and bad breath. Good oral hygiene includes regular brushing and flossing, using mouthwash, and routine visits to the dentist for cleanings and check-ups. Maintaining proper oral hygiene is essential for overall health and well-being.

  • Live Advisory

    Live Advisory offers real-time dental consultations where you can get expert advice from our dental professionals. Whether you have a quick question or need guidance on a dental issue, our team is available to provide personalized support and answers to your concerns.

  • Cavity Inspection

    Cavity inspection involves a thorough examination of your teeth to detect any signs of tooth decay. Using advanced tools and techniques, we check for cavities and assess the health of your teeth to ensure early detection and treatment, helping to maintain your oral health.
